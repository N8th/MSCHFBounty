Submit a player's name and a small fee to put them on the bounty board. Your submission will be anonymous, and you can include a message to persuade others. The bounty board refreshes daily, giving you the chance to steer the game and protect yourself.

How It Works

  1. Worried about being voted out? Submit a bounty on another player by paying $1 through Venmo to @MSCHFBounty along with the name of your target.
  2. Include an anonymous message explaining why others should vote for this player.
  3. The bounty and message will appear on the bounty board for everyone to see.
  4. Other players, seeing the bounty, may decide to vote for that player instead of you.

This system helps you stay in the game longer by directing attention to other players.
